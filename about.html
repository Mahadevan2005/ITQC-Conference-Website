About India-Thai Quantum Conference 2025

The India-Thai Quantum Conference 2025 (ITQC) is a unique platform to explore the groundbreaking advancements in quantum technologies. This prestigious event aims to bridge the gap between researchers, students, and professionals in India and Thailand to drive global innovation.

Hosted at Prince Shri Venkateshwara Engineering College, ITQC 2025 will serve as a hub for knowledge-sharing, collaboration, and networking in the fast-evolving field of quantum computing and communication.
