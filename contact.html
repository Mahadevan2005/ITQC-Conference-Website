Prince Shri Venkateshwara Padmavathy Engineering College

Prince Shri Venkateshwara Padmavathy Engineering College (PSVPEC) is committed to pioneering advancements in technology and research. With a vision to contribute to the nation's growth, PSVPEC is dedicated to exploring the immense potential of quantum computing, aligning with national efforts such as the National Mission on Quantum Technologies & Applications (NMQTA).

Our Efforts in Quantum Computing

PSVPEC is proud to support and contribute to the development of quantum technologies. The college has partnered with research institutions and actively participates in projects aimed at advancing quantum computing education and research. Students and faculty members are continuously exploring innovative approaches to integrate quantum technologies into practical applications.
