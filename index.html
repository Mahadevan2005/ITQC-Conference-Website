What is Quantum Computing?

Quantum computing leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to process information in fundamentally new ways. Unlike classical computers, which use bits, quantum computers use qubits, allowing them to solve complex problems at unprecedented speeds.

By attending ITQC 2025, you'll gain insights into the latest breakthroughs in quantum technology, connect with thought leaders, and explore practical applications in fields such as cryptography, AI, finance, and healthcare.
